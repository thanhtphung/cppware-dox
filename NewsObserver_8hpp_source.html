17 class NewsObserver: public Observer
18  //!
19  //! A class representing an observer in the observer design pattern. An observer can attach
20  //! to and detach from an observable subject. When an observable subject is updated, its
21  //! observers are notified. This implementation requires that both observable subjects and
22  //! observers are reference counted. To help troubleshooting, both can be named with free-format
23  //! texts, and some counters (update count, notify count, etc.) are provided. With the default
24  //! Observer class, there's no built-in support for attaching to an observable at availability.
25  //! With this NewsObserver class, an observer can attach to a named observable as soon as it
26  //! is available. Also, the observer can follow the named observable as it comes and goes.
27  //!
