16  //! A class representing a section of critical code which cannot be used by
17  //! multiple threads concurrently. Before entering a critical section, it must
18  //! be locked to prevent other threads from concurrent used. After exiting a
19  //! critical section, it must be unlocked to allow its use by other threads.
20  //! A critical section should be locked/unlocked by contructing/destructing a
